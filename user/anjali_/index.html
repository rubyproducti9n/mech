Introduction

Sanjivani College of Engineering (An Autonomous Institute), Kopargaon is one among the premier technical institutes in Maharashtra state in the un-aided sector. Established in 1983 by the Sanjivani Rural Educational Society under the charismatic leadership of Shri. Shankarrao G. Kolhe, the Institute had its vision for social transformation and , upliftment of rural masses through education, training and research.

College Canteen

There is one cafeteria and two canteens available in college campus which provides the snacks, lunch and dinner at an affordable costs. The College Management has set norms for the food quality which is checked at time intervals. The College has banned sell of packaged food material in order to keep the campus plastic-free.

Sports

The College has a spacious Gymkhana well equipped with all facilities for indoor games like Badminton, Table Tennis, Wrestling, Carom and Chess. The College has spacious playgrounds for following outdoor games: Basketball. Volley Ball.
